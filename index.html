About Me

Hey! My name is Edward and I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.

My journey in computer science began when I developed my own deep learning model to diagnose tuberculosis in chest x-rays. I presented this at the 2018 Southern California Conference for Undergraduate Research and was recruited by Huntington Medical Research Insitutes' Neuroimaging Lab to work on a project where I segmented brain magnetic resonance images for white matter lesions. In April 2019, I won a research award after presenting at the Honors Transfer Council of California Conference. Ever since then, I've been exploring different fields in which I could apply artificial intelligence to. Recently, I started getting into full stack development.


When I'm not coding, I love solving rubik's cubes, playing guitar, watching kdramas, training in boxing, and exploring new cooking recipes! One fun fact about me is that I'm a skincare model for Lalana International in Thailand. I really enjoy meeting new people. I'm always down to grab food, get boba, or go on a hike! Feel free to message me about anything!
